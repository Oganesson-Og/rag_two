Basic Algebra:
1. The quadratic formula:
   x = (-b +/- sqrt(b^2 - 4ac)) / 2a

2. Expansion of (a + b)^2:
   (a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2

3. Factoring a difference of squares:
   a^2 - b^2 = (a - b)(a + b)

Calculus:
1. Derivative of a polynomial:
   d/dx(ax^n) = nax^(n-1)

2. Indefinite integral:
   Integral(x^n dx) = (x^(n+1)) / (n+1) + C (for n != -1)

3. Chain rule:
   d/dx[f(g(x))] = f'(g(x)) * g'(x)

Trigonometry:
1. Pythagorean identity:
   sin^2(theta) + cos^2(theta) = 1

2. Double angle formulas:
   sin(2theta) = 2 * sin(theta) * cos(theta)
   cos(2theta) = cos^2(theta) - sin^2(theta)

Linear Algebra:
1. Matrix multiplication rule:
   (AB)_(ij) = sum(A_(ik) * B_(kj), k=1 to n)

2. Determinant of a 2x2 matrix:
   det([[a, b], [c, d]]) = ad - bc

Physics Equations:
1. Newton's second law:
   F = ma

2. Equation of motion:
   s = ut + (1/2)at^2

3. Einstein's mass-energy equivalence:
   E = mc^2
